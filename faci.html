  • Conference Hall

Administrative Facilities
  • Administrative area includes Principal’s Office, Chairman Cabin, Main Office, Reception Counter & waiting foyer.
  • Fully Computerized Office administration with Internet Connectivity.
  • Dedicated, well experinced and qualified Office Staff.
  • Administrative Office having separate sections like Establishement Section, Student Section, Account Section and Scholarship Section.
Hostel Facilities
  • Well furnished, spacious, triple Occupancy Rooms are available for both BOYS and GIRLS separtely having 60 seats capacity each.

  • Mess facility available for both Boys & Girls Hostellers and others.

  • Workshop have sections with well equipped latest machinery like Machine Shop, Welding, Fitting, Carpentry / Pattern Making, Foundry, Smithy, Plumbing, Sheet Metal with skilled and qualified Instructors.

EDP Cell

Entrepreneur & Development Cell

The EDP Cell of Shivaji Polytechnic College has been established in 2016-17 for enhance the idea & to encourage the spirit of entrepreneurship amongst the youth. This is very essential for economic growth of the country. The Cell is an effective platform for the students to develop the entrepreneurial attitude as it arranges several development programs. Through which they provided with an opportunity to meet successful Entrepreneurs. EDC cell provides an opportunity to translate the innovative ideas of the students to functional business setups. Provides the students with an opportunity to develop socially and economically through setting up their own enterprise


EDP Activities

  1. Encouraging students to become an Entrepreneur..
  2. Improve skills and qualities required to become good entrepreneurs.
  3. Conducting various EDP programs & To conduct entrepreneurship Awareness camps.
  4. Conducting soft skill development program for students.
  5. Guidance for preparation of business project.
  6. Alumni, who have established their own Enterprise are invited to share their experiences with the students regularly..
  7. Industrial visits are arranged for the Staff and Students.
  8. To provide exposure to various aspects of entrepreneurship with the help of both in house and guest facilities.
  9. To provide an interaction with successful entrepreneurs in different fields of industry.
  10. Guest lecture on Entrepreneurship Development.

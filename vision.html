About Trust
This Polytechnic College is the first institute established by Ramkrishna Technical Education Society's (RTES) in the year 2009.It offers Three Year (Six semesters) Diploma courses in Engineering & Technology in Civil Engg. , Computer Engg. , Electronics & Telecommunication Engg.,& Mechanical Engg. It has set a tradition of excellent results with all round development of students. Since its inception it has been a rich source of competent engineers for the industry. The Shivaji Polytechnic College is fully equipped with modern infrastructure such as spacious lecture halls with modern audio-visual aids, fully developed laboratories, library, reading rooms, seminar hall and computer labs. It has qualified and experienced faculty that makes the backbone to the institute
About Institute
This polytechnic college is the first institute established by Ramkrishna Technical Education Society's (RTES) in the year 2009. It offers Three Year (Six semesters) Diploma courses in Engineering & Technology in Civil Engg., Computer Engg., Electronics & Telecommunication Engg., Information Technology & Mechanical Engg. It has set a tradition of excellent results with all round development of students. Since its inception it has been a rich source of competent engineers for the industry. The Shivaji Polytechnic College is fully equipped with modern infrastructure such as spacious lecture halls with modern audio-visual aids, fully developed laboratories, library, reading rooms, seminar halls and computer labs. It has qualified and experienced faculty that makes the backbone to the institute.

Vision & Mission

Vision
To impart quality technical education based on values to the students of draught prone regions to create technocrats and entrepreneurs to meet needs of industry and society at large.
Mission
Our mission is to create an environment in which students will be able to realize technical and entrepreneurship potential through students and industry centric pedagogy.
Objectives
  • To strive for excellence in academic & research in order to achieve proficiency in students by improving teaching Learning process.
  • To enhance career opportunities for students through exposure to industry.
  • To promote all round development of students.
  • To enhance innovation & creativity of students & faculty.

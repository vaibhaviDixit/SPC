About Us

This Polytechnic College is the first institute established by RTES in the year 2009.It offers Three Year (Six semesters) Diploma courses in Engineering & Technology in Civil Engg.,Computer Engg.,Electronics & Telecommunication Engg.,& Mechanical Engg. It has set a tradition of excellent results with all round development of students. Since its inception it has been a rich source of competent engineers for the industry. The Shivaji Polytechnic College is fully equipped with modern infrastructure such as spacious lecture halls with modern audio-visual aids, fully developed laboratories, library, reading rooms, seminar hall and computer labs. It has qualified and experienced faculty that makes the backbone to the institute..
